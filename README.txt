Team Name: Picme
Repository Link: https://github.com/picme-app/picme-app.github.io
Site Link: https://picme-app.github.io/

Project Description:
This app allows you to connect with photographers in your area, on demand,
for a quick photoshoot. Professional and hobbyist photographers can make a
bit of money in their spare time while helping tourists and the local community.

Features

PicMe is a service that allows people to get in contact with a photograph near their area for a quick photoshoot. Customers have the option of selecting a professional photograph with an already established studio or an amateur whoâ€™s just starting out.

With the phone app, the user can locate and choose a photograph signed up with the service and have them get to their location in a few minutes.

After the shoot, the photographer sends the pictures to the customers through the PicMe app.

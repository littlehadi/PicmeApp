Overall Look and Feel of Picme
==============================

The overall look will be minimalist and flat, following the current trend in style. We will avoid anything related to 3D
and gradients. The website should feel professional and not hurt the reader's eyes

Colours
========

- #F00000
- #B00000
- #640000
- #710000
- #4A0000
- #222222
- #333333
- #444444

Iconography
===========
We will be using Font Awesome 5 as a base for our icons.

Typography
==========
We will be using Titillium Web from Google Fonts as the website's main font.

Links
=====
Links will not be underlined and should become lighter when the user hovers over them with their mouse.

Buttons
=======
Buttons will be flat designed using the chosen colors outlined above.
